Step 05

About ITD

The B.S. in Innovation, Technology, and Design is a multidisciplinary, university-wide program at the University of Miami that prepares students for careers at the intersection of design, technology, and innovation.

ITD emphasizes hands-on, cross-disciplinary learning and helps students build future-proof skills through creative problem-solving, collaboration, and real-world making.
